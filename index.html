Ever wondered if you're the only person in the whole world reading a web page?

Chances are, in a wonderful moment of serendipity, there may be another soul, somewhere on this planet, also reading the same page as you are, at the very same moment.

Take a look at the bottom of this page.

This is turven, a tiny widget I built that you can include on your website. It's open-source, easy to install, and available to all.

May you find yourself surrounded by kind Internet strangers... and if no else is around, share this page to a friend and watch the counter go up :)
